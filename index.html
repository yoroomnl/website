Policy

Cancellation You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before it starts.

To ensure the best experience for everyone, review the guidelines:

Please aim to arrive 8-10 minutes before class starts. This gives you plenty of time to stow your belongings and settle onto your mat without rushing.

I have a few yoga mats available in studio. However, for hygiene purposes, I highly recommend bringing your own mat if possible.

I suggest bringing a small towel and a bottle of water to stay comfortable and hydrated during your practice.

If you need to cancel, please do so at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee or loss of class credit.

Thank you! 🙏
